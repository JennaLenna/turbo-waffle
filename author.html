I Write Books!

It's always funny to me, because most people see me as an artist first an an author second, but I typically see myself as the opposite! I've been writing "since I could write my name" (quote from my mom). I still remember the day I opened a blank notebook at 5 years old and began writing out shaky letters with a rainbow crayon. Nowadays, my entire approach to writing looks a lot different: I rotate between typing and writing on paper, and I do extensive character planning and worldbuilding. There's something magical about being able to do essentially anything with some words, and being able to bring the stories that already live in my head to life is too amazing of an opportunity to pass up.
